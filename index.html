The Challenge in Rural Kenya

70% of Kenya's clinics are in rural areas with limited access to labs, specialists, and reliable diagnostics.

2-3 Day Lab Wait

Patients travel hours to urban labs, then wait days for results. Many never return.

High Lab Costs

KSh 500-2,000 per test + transport. Unaffordable for most rural families.

Missed Diagnoses

Without rapid testing, early-stage diseases go undetected, leading to preventable deaths.

Our Solution: Phone + Test Kit + AI

We integrate off-the-shelf hardware with proprietary AI to deliver lab-quality diagnostics at the point of care.
